# Emergency Policy

We prioritise emergency and critical patients above all scheduled appointments.

If you arrive with an emergency, please advise reception immediately. Your pet will be assessed by a nurse within minutes.

## What counts as an emergency

- Difficulty breathing
- Collapse or loss of consciousness
- Suspected poisoning (snake, toad, chocolate, xylitol, grapes, etc.)
- Seizure
- Inability to urinate, especially in male cats
- Pale, blue, or grey gums
- Uncontrolled bleeding
- Hit by a car or serious trauma
- Bloated or distended abdomen with distress

## After-hours emergencies

We are not open 24 hours. If you have an emergency outside our opening hours, please go directly to:
