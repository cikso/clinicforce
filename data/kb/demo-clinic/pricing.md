# Pricing

We do not publish a full price list as costs vary depending on your pet's condition and the treatment required.

## Consultation Fees (indicative)

- Standard consultation: from $90
- Emergency consultation (during hours): from $150
- After-hours emergency (partner clinic): fees set by the emergency centre

## Payment Plans

We offer Vet Pay and Afterpay to help spread the cost of unexpected veterinary bills. Ask reception for details.

## Pet Insurance

We accept all major pet insurance providers. You will pay upfront and claim with your insurer. We can provide itemised invoices for claims.

## Estimates

We will always provide a written estimate before proceeding with any procedure. You are never committed to treatment without your approval.
