# Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need an appointment?**
Appointments are preferred but walk-ins are welcome. Emergency cases are always seen immediately.

**How do I book?**
You can book by phone on (03) 9123 4567 or online through our website.

**Do you treat rabbits and birds?**
Yes. We treat dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, guinea pigs, and small exotic animals.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept cash, EFTPOS, Visa, Mastercard, Afterpay, and Vet Pay payment plans.

**Can I get a prescription diet or medication without a consult?**
Prescription items require a current consultation record. If your pet has been seen recently, call us and we can advise.

**Do you offer home visits?**
We do not currently offer home visits.

**What if my pet needs care overnight?**
We will stabilise and refer to our after-hours emergency partner if overnight care is needed outside our hours.
