# Services We Offer

- Emergency and critical care
- General consultations
- Vaccinations (puppy, kitten, adult, and senior)
- Desexing (dogs and cats)
- Dental procedures including scale and polish
- Microchipping
- X-ray and ultrasound imaging
- In-house blood and urine pathology
- Soft tissue and orthopaedic surgery
- Prescription diets and medications

# Species We Treat

We treat dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, guinea pigs, and small exotic animals.
We do not treat livestock, horses, or large farm animals.
